COURIERMAILING Group is one of the world’s leading logistic /Security company. The Group connects people and markets and is an enabler of global trade. It aspires to be the first choice for customers, employees and investors worldwide.

To this end, COURIERMAILING Group is focusing on growth in its profitable core logistics businesses and accelerating the digital transformation in all business divisions.

The Group contributes to the world through sustainable business practices, corporate citizenship and environmental activities. By the year 2050, COURIERMAILING Group aims to achieve net-zero emissions logistics.
